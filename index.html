Controls

  • WASD - Move
  • Mouse - Shoot
  • Hold Shift to aim
  • Mouse Scroll - Navigate Inventory
  • E - Use item
  • Q - Drop item
  • 1,2,3,4 - Change skin
  • 5,6,7,8 - Change gun
  • Anykey - Skip intro
  • F7 - Save (After ship has crashed)
  • F9 - Load
